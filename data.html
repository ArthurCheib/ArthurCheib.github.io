Freedom in the World Index

The analyzed dataset is the Freedom in the World Index, a reputable data set developed by Freedom House. The goal was to better understand global trends in Freedom and democracy over the 21st century. The data revealed an unexpected trend: Freedom has steadily declined in stable democracies throughout the 21st century. Political rights and civil liberties have experienced a downward trajectory in countries typically regarded as bastions of democracy. The data suggests that this trend is not a temporary anomaly but a persistent pattern.

The study employed a regression on observables to dissect the effects of factors such as time in service, education level, and job role.
