Welcome to my website!

I am a dedicated professional in public policy and data analysis. Hailing from Brazil, I bring a diverse background and a passion for improving data-driven decision-making within the public sector.

Before joining the University of Chicago for my Master of Public Policy ('23), I worked in my country for public and non-profit sectors with data visualization, analysis, and modeling.

Currently, I work as a consultant for the World Bank at the Education Global Practice, and as an Instructor of the Coding Lab for Public Policy (PPHA 30310) at the University of Chicago.
