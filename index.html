Welcome to my website!

I am a dedicated professional in public policy and data analysis. Hailing from Minas Gerais (Brazil), I bring a diverse background and a passion for improving data-driven decision-making processes within the public sector.

Before joining the University of Chicago for my Master of Public Policy ('23 class), I worked in my home country for public and non-profit sectors with data cleaning, visualization, analysis, and modeling.

Currently, I work as a consultant for the World Bank, specifically within the Education Global Practice, and as an Instructor of the Coding Lab for Public Policy (PPHA 30310) at the University of Chicago.
